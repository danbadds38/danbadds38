## 🧬 Who I am

Seasoned **DevSecOps leader**, **cloud architect**, and **entrepreneur** on a mission to make robust cybersecurity accessible to organisations of every size — from SMBs to governments.

Over a decade securing Fortune 100 companies, scaling global platforms, and leading high-performing engineering teams across some of the highest-stakes environments in finance and technology — including the **NYSE**, **NASDAQ**, and **FS-ISAC** threat intelligence repositories.
